COMMUNITY INTEREST ANNUAL REPORT

GET SET 4 TENNIS CIC

Company Number: 11536392 (England and Wales)

Year Ending: 31 August 2019

Company activities and impact

Get Set 4 Tennis CIC launched its community tennis club in August 2018 with its emphasis on inclusive, affordable tennis. During the period to 31 August 2019, the club has benefitted the community in the following ways:* Providing a structured set of group lessons run by professionally trained tennis coaches. Lessons are on a pay as you go basis to keep the cost for the community low* Local schools and nursery children have been invited to join these groups through out talent ID system, as well as having an open doors policy to the surrounding community.* We have run social and community events including an inclusive tennis festival, awards evening and many more to make tennis as accessible as possible.* We have launched disability tennis and inclusive tennis in 14 local schools (9 SEN and 5 mainstream primary who have a high level of SEN children) and introduced over 1300 children to the game of tennis either by grant funded or Get Set 4 Tennis funded lessons
