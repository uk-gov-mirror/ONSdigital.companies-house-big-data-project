Company activities and impact

Project Paul CIC exists to promote positive activities for children, young people and adults within our community. During 2018-2019 our activities included :

Consultation with stakeholders

The primary stakeholders are the children, young people and their families who attended our workshops, sessions and activities, or were consequential beneficiaries of the operational solutions we implemented . Other stakeholders included our adult and young volunteers and our service collaborators including schools/ workshop leaders and our funders.
